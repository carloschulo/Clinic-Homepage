Little Titans is an occupational therapy facility whose primary population of service will be to children between the ages of 5 - 21 with varying disabilities, disorders and conditions (e.g. Cerebral Palsy, Down Syndrome and Autism Spectrum Disorder). Our purpose is to provide family-centered, evidence-based therapy using sensory treatment and aqua therapy in order to help children with disabilities learn to regulate their own sensory needs to function at their greatest capacity in their everyday lives.

Little Titans can be found in the heart of Brooklyn in Kensington. Two blocks from Church Avenue Station and a walking distance from Prospect Park and other attractions, it is conveniently located in a diverse, family-friendly, and easily accessible community. Our site consists of a sensory gym, a sensory/quiet room, and an indoor pool that will be used interchangeably for the individualized treatment plan of each child and their needs.
